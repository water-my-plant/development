Water My Plants

Introducing the app to keep your plants happy and healthy even when you have a busy schedule!

Keep a convenient log of all your plant babies on the homepage, and easily add or delete plants at anytime.

Add a new plant by adding the type or name of the plant, the species, and it's watering schedule to keep your plant happy!

Sign up to login and start creating a watering schedule that helps your plants thrive! You can customize your user name and change your phone number at anytime.
